About Me

My story began on September 5th of 2003. In the busy delivery rooms of Mary Chiles General Hospital Manila, I was born. I was raised in a Catholic oriented huosehold in Makati, lived with my five older siblings and my parents named Jose and Angela Tenorio.


I studied pre-school to 3rd Grade at Francisco School of Makati. Which all of my siblings attended to during their early childhood. I transferred to Hen. Pio Del Pilar Elementary School 1, when my sister started working there as a teacher for Primary students.


One of the most memorable experiences I had when I was a childhood was very diverse. I really like going out and so, whenever my siblings can tag me along with their errands I always end up enjoying and find is as one of the greatest adventures during childhood. I guess that's the perks of being the youngest.


Now, I find going out very meaningful. As a young adult, I am to discover more about the world I live in. No more sugarcoating though as the child at my heart would be hurt. I am to discover more about the world around me, and it is fascinating to appreciate it in detail. Now, I find going out meaningful with other people as I can understand things in a new and different perspective knowing that I'm living a different world from theirs and it's just wonderful.
